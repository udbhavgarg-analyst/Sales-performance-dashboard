Sales Performance Dashboard
 Project Overview:-
This project analyzes sales data to identify revenue drivers, category performance, country trends, and determine whether growth is volume-driven or price-driven.

Tools & Technologies:-
SQL (Data Cleaning & Aggregation)
Power BI (Dashboard Development)
DAX (MoM Growth, ASP).

Business Questions Solved:-
Which countries and categories drive the highest revenue?
Is revenue growth driven by quantity or price?
Is revenue concentrated in a few categories (Pareto 80/20 rule)?
What are the monthly growth trends?

Key Insights:-
Revenue growth is primarily volume-driven, as quantity trends closely follow revenue trends.
The top 3 categories contribute nearly 75–80% of total revenue, indicating concentration risk.
Certain markets behave as premium (higher ASP, lower order volume).
Seasonal spikes observed in mid-year months.

Business Recommendations:-

Increase marketing focus on high-performing categories.
Monitor ASP trends to optimize pricing strategies.
Diversify revenue sources to reduce category concentration risk.
Align inventory planning with seasonal demand patterns.
